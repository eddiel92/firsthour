FirstHour Privacy Policy

FirstHour V1 is local-only. Your wake settings, QR tag, reflection notes, and completion history stay on this device.

Local Data

FirstHour stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Daily wake time and morning label
  • QR tag label and local tag token
  • Prompt pack selection
  • Completion records
  • Reflection text

Data is stored with Codable JSON in the app's local Application Support directory.

No Backend, Accounts, Or Cloud Sync

FirstHour V1 has no backend, no accounts, no cloud sync, and no server uploads. Data does not leave the device unless you choose to share a tag or proof card through the native iOS share sheet.

V1 Exclusions

FirstHour V1 does not use AI, image recognition, partner notifications, HealthKit, step tracking, subscriptions, payments, Screen Time, FamilyControls, private APIs, or system-level app blocking.

FirstHour V1 uses iOS notifications as wake prompts. Keep your normal alarm enabled if you need a guaranteed alarm.
